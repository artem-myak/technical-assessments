# Technical Assessments & Analytical Case Studies

This repository contains a collection of business cases and technical tasks solved as part of recruitment processes for Data Analyst roles. Each project represents a real-world business challenge, ranging from funnel optimization to financial modeling.

## ⚠️ Data Privacy & Confidentiality
To respect the intellectual property and confidentiality of the companies involved:
* All original company names have been anonymized.
* Financial and sensitive business metrics have been scaled or modified.
* Any proprietary internal logic or specific business terminology has been replaced with generalized industry standards.
